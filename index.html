Practical information

Keukenhof is open daily from 08:00 – 19:30 from 24 March – 15 May.

Tickets can be bought via www.keukenhof.nl. Tickets can only be purchased online. The tickets are valid on a specific date during the opening season with arrival expected during a predetermined timeslot.

Flowering at Keukenhof parallels that in nature. The crocuses, daffodils, hyacinths and early tulips flower at the start of the season. Other flowers bloom later in the season such as the (larger) tulips. It is less busy before 10:30 and after 16:00. It is also less busy on Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday than on the other days of the week.

Currently there are no corona measures for a day out in the Netherlands. Therefore you do not need a COVID passport to visit Keukenhof. We do ask you to be considerate of each other and keep your distance when possible. Keukenhof can receive a maximum number of visitors per day. We have implemented timeslots to prevent busy times during the day.
